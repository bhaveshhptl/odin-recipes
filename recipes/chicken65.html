Chicken 65

Description

Ingredients

  • Boneless pieces
  • Yogurt
  • Red chili powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Garam masala
  • Curry leaves
  • Cornflour
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Green chilies (optional, for extra spiciness)
  • Cilantro (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Marination:
    • Combine chicken, yogurt, red chili powder, garlic paste, ginger paste, cumin powder, coriander leaves, and salt in a mixing bowl.
    • Coat the chicken evenly with the marinade.
    • Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or preferably overnight.
  2. Batter Preparation:
    • In a separate bowl, whisk together egg white, rice flour, cornflour, all-purpose flour, and a pinch of salt.
    • Adjust the consistency to form a moderately thick batter.
  3. Frying:
    • Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
    • Dip the marinated chicken pieces in the batter, ensuring a complete coating.
    • Gently slide the battered chicken into the hot oil.
    • Fry until they turn crispy and golden brown, approximately 4-5 minutes.
    • Remove from oil and drain excess oil on a wire rack or paper towels.
  4. Tempering:
    • In a separate pan, heat 1-2 tbsp of oil.
    • Add chopped garlic, green chilies, and curry leaves. Saut√© until fragrant.
  5. Tossing:
    • Transfer the fried chicken pieces to the pan with the tempered oil.
    • Gently toss to coat the chicken with the aromatic oil and spices.
    • Sprinkle black pepper powder and garnish with additional curry leaves, if desired.
  6. Serving:
    • Serve Chicken 65 immediately, accompanied by your preferred chutney or sauce.
    • Lemon wedges enhance the flavors and provide a refreshing contrast.
